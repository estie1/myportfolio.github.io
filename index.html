About Me

Software Developer

I have always been fascinated by technology and the way it interacts with the world around us as well as its ability to affect communities positively. My drive stems from the desire to create impactful software that solves real-world problems and enhances the user experience. I recognised my ability to work with computers early on as well as my interest in design, so I did well in IT and Graphic design in my GCSEs. Continuing this to A levels in college. I was unable to go beyond year 1, due to pressures at the time.

After my studies I ventured into Health and Social Care as I desired to support the vulnerable and immediately recognised the advantage of my IT, Design and Research skills as it made my work efficient and well presented. I am confident the skills I have obtained from working with families (some who can be challenging), social work teams and other professionals from other organisations and the use of technologies will serve me well in any setting that I become a part of. On the side I was using website creation platforms such as Squarespace and Word Press to design websites and blogs for people to help them out with their online representation.

At the end of last year 2022, I decided to take the step and embark on completing a Software Development Skills bootcamp with JustIt, to start pursuing a career in this field. I am very grateful for the opportunity to learn more about coding and programming.
